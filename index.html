Scottish Mfg

This is my first d3 sketch. Still learning! Plus this could be a lot cleaner.

Graph shows percent year-over-year growth for the Scottish manufacturing sector, shown quarterly. Above 100% means Scottish manufacturing is growing. Below 100% and it's shrinking. Click the button for the breakdown by manufacturing sub-sectors.

Data comes from Scottish GDP figures and my own calculations. First produced for research funded by the US-UK Fulbright Commission and the University of Strathclyde.
